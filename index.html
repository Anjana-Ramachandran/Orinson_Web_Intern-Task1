About Us

At Fork & Fire, we're passionate about serving up delicious, crafted comfort food that brings people together. Our chefs combine traditional flavors with creative twists to create mouthwatering burgers, wood-fired pizzas, and artisanal pasta dishes that will leave you craving more.

Our Story

Fork & Fire was born from a love of good food, warm gatherings, and community. Our founders, Anaswara and Anjana, wanted to create a cozy spot where friends and family could share laughs, make memories, and savor every bite.
